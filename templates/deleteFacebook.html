Data Deletion Instructions

If you'd like to delete your data or revoke permissions related to our application, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your Facebook account.
  2. Go to Settings & Privacy > Settings.
  3. In the left column, click on "Apps and Websites".
  4. You'll see a list of apps and websites you've used Facebook to log into. Find our app in the list.
  5. Click on our app's name to view the permissions you've granted.
  6. To revoke permissions, click "Remove" next to the permissions you'd like to revoke.
  7. If you'd like to completely delete your account from our app, click "Remove" next to our app's name.
  8. Follow the prompts to confirm your action.
